Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

Images, text talking about the importance of a balanced diet, maybe a video or two.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

Most likely off of the internet, if need be I'll take my own pictures/do research myself.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

A balanced diet is very important to overall health and to an extent the quality of life of a person.

Why should a site be built for this?

There are many websites built around the idea of a balanced diet, however, there are still many out there that do not take what they eat seriously.

What would you like users to do at your site?

Hopefully learn about the importance of a balanced diet, and improve their diet accordingly.

What is the goal of the site?

To improve the dietary habits of the users that view the website.
