Website Summary:

What content do competing websites have?

Competing websites have images of people before and after dieting, much information about what the optimal diet is for what demographic, and some general diet habit information.>

What is the competition doing right?

Giving the users a sense of ease in knowing what they are doing right, helping them use the website easier and making their experience on the website better.

What is the competition doing wrong?

They spend too much time on the good, while not talking about the detriments of not following a healthy diet.>

How can I do better?

Inform users about the potential good that can be achieved by following a healthy, balanced diet, while also keeping users in the know of what the detriments could be should they not follow a healty diet.
